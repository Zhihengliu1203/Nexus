Our mission: Make sports more accessible, easier to organize, and more social for students
Why Nexus

Less organizing. More energy.

The issue is not a lack of interest in sport. It is the difficulty of coordinating, finding people, and making the activity easy to launch.

🤝

Meet people

Join an activity even if you come alone and connect with other students who want to play.

🗓️

Play more easily

More clarity on groups, schedules, and available time slots, with less unnecessary organizing.

💸

Share costs

When fees are split between several players, it becomes easier to play regularly.

From the idea to the court

A simple path to take action.

1

Choose your sport

Find the activity or time slot that matches your energy, your level, and your schedule.

2

Join or create

Join an existing group or create your own in a few clicks.

3

Confirm and play

Once the right group comes together, the activity moves from idea to the court.

A simple and clear model

Pricing designed to stay flexible at launch.

Pay per activity

Flexible depending on the activity

Transparent coordination fees are added to the activity to simplify organization, matchmaking, and the overall experience.

  • ideal for testing the platform
  • no long-term commitment
  • perfect for occasional play

Nexus Membership

Scalable depending on the benefits offered

A plan designed for more regular users, with potential benefits on certain time slots, priorities, or rewards.

  • better for frequent players
  • access to member benefits
  • a more sustainable vision of the sports community
